Who we are

In Italy, an oste is
more than a host

It's the person who welcomes you to their table, shares the stories of their land, and opens doors you'd never find alone.

At OSTE, that's exactly what we do. Every journey is personally crafted with trusted local partners — chefs, winemakers, artisans, and storytellers — so you don't just visit Italy, you're welcomed into its heart.

With carefully chosen companions and intimate group sizes, each itinerary blends discovery and downtime, tradition and comfort, intimacy and inspiration.

What we stand for

Authenticity

Real people, real stories, real Italy. No manufactured moments, no tourist traps.

Intimacy

Maximum 8 guests per journey. Small enough to feel like a private experience.

Warmth

Hospitality that feels like being welcomed home. We host, we don't just guide.

Heritage

Deep respect for local culture, craft, and tradition. Italy as Italians live it.
